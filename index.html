Vikas ki Gatha

Ramnagar ka itihaas 400 varshon se bhi purana hai. Iski sthapna yahan ke mool-nivasiyon ne ki thi. Gaon ne kai utaar-chadhav dekhe hain, par apni pehchan barkarar rakhi hai. Aaj, yeh aadhunik suvidhaon aur paramparik moolyon ka mishran hai.

Bhaugolik Saundarya

Narmada Nadi ke pavitra tat par sthit, Ramnagar charon dishaon se ghane jangalon aur upjau khetaron se ghira hai. Yahan ka mausam saalon bhar suhavana rehta hai, aur prakritik saundarya man moh leta hai.

Paramparik Utsav

Holi, Diwali, Gangaur, aur Krishna Janmashtami yahan ke pramukh tyohar hain. Gaonwale ekjut hokar in utsavon ko bade ullas se manate hain. Lok-nritya aur paramparik bhojan in tyoharon ki jaan hain.

Gramin Shiksha Kendra

Hamare gaon mein ek suvyavasthit Primary aur Middle School hai, jismein 300 se adhik vidyarthi shiksha grahan karte hain. Yahan computer lab aur library ki suvidha bhi uplabdh hai, jo bhavishya ke nirmataon ko taiyar kar rahi hai.

Adarsh Panchayat Bhawan

Naya nirmit Panchayat Bhawan gaon ke prashasan ka kendra hai. Yahan par regular Gram Sabha aayojit hoti hai, jismein gaon ke vikas aur samasyaon par charcha ki jaati hai. Yeh sthan gaon ki ekta aur pragati ka prateek hai.

Prathmik Swasthya Kendra

Gaon mein ek Prathmik Swasthya Kendra (Dispensary) hai jahan ek anubhavi doctor aur nurse niyamit roop se uparsthit rehte hain. Yahin par chote-mote rogon ka ilaj aur vaccination camps lagaye jaate hain.
